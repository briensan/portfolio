About

Where the books and the data meet

My background started in accounting: audit support, tax preparation, and financial analysis across firms including Grant Thornton and Intuit Canada. Along the way I kept finding that the most useful part of the job was making sense of the numbers, not just recording them — so I'm now formalizing that instinct with a Graduate Diploma in Data Analytics for Business.

I'm comfortable moving between a general ledger and a SQL query, and I care about the same thing in both: accuracy that someone can build a decision on. I'm currently interviewing for accounting, finance, and analytics-adjacent roles across Canada.
